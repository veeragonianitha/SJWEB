Create Your Custom ETF Portfolio

This is a feature to create your desired ETF Portfolio of stocks and getting other users opinion,which will help you to delete,add certain stocks from your ETF portfolio,in order to construct a desired portfolio based on your needs and be confident about your financial health.

How it works

1.Give your portfolio Name

2.Define purpose such as High yields for Retirement,Long term 10 years,5 years,2 years short term or dividends

3.Add stocks to your portfolio by typing stock names or Ticker,against each stock add optional note why you choose this stock

4.Preview the post before you submit to see,how it look like when you post for user opinion.Your defined ETF will give value based on current stock prices,which will be useful for tracking its performance.

5.Post to public or your specific group for getting opinion.
